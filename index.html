About

After completing my studies as Environmental Engineer at the University of Panama I started working in the field of agroforestry in a small company in Panama. Then I moved to Germany with the aim of studying and working in the field of remote sensing. After completing my Master's Degree at the Technical University of Munich, I worked in the remote sensing industry primarily with optical and radar images from the Sentinel missions. As an autodidact, I learned the languages Python and C to automate routines at work. Currently, I am focused in the field of remote sensing for agriculture.
